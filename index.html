THE ROASTERS VILLAGE & CUPPING ZONE

The Roasters Village & Cupping Zone, curated by Maxwell Colonna-Dashwood, is all about bringing the industry’s best coffees, people and companies together into one space.

We want to create a dynamic space that allows and encourages visitors to sample and focus on incredible coffee.

calling all coffee lovers

Calling all coffee lovers – want to join the Ukrainian Coffee Festival Team?

We’re looking for passionate, reliable and enthusiastic individuals to volunteer at the Ukrainian Coffee Festival 2019.
A wider range of volunteer opportunities are on offer in 2019 than ever before, from half-day general festival runner roles to 3-day Senior Volunteer positions guaranteeing front-line Event Management & Coffee Industry experience and insight.
In exchange for volunteering at the festival, you will receive a hands-on experience with full access, outside of your volunteer shift, to explore the festival!
So however, you take your coffee, if you’d like to volunteer at the first Ukrainian Coffee Festival, fill out the form and we’ll be in touch soon!
