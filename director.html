Directors

Connor Clarke

Managing Director

Connor Clarke was born in Keelung, Taiwan and raised in the Mid-West of the United States.

Him start in the coffee industry began with a position as a barista 1993, soon after, him fascination with the coffee industry grew into a passionate hobby and dedicated career. In 1998, he joined Counter Culture Coffee, where he further developed him interests and focus in sustainable coffee, business management, education, and specialty coffee promotion.

Connor has dedicated a number of volunteer hours on projects with the Specialty Coffee Association of America (SCAA), serving at times as an instructor and committee member. He became the Regional Coordinator of the Americas for the World Barista Championship (WBC) in 2005 and joined the organization’s board of directors in 2007. Connor was appointed as the Executive Director of the WBC in 2009, and later, Managing Director of World Coffee Events (WCE) in 2011.

Amy Ball

Financial Manager

Amy lives and works in Dublin, Ireland and has worked with World Coffee Events Limited since 2011. A member of the Institute of Chartered Accountants in Ireland since 1985, having trained with Ernst & Young, Irene then worked with E&Y Toronto for 3 years.

Since returning to Dublin, in addition to working in Practice, Amy has worked with a number of private Irish companies including Marco Beverage Systems Limited.

As well as a B.A. in Economics and a Higher Diploma in Psychology from University College Dublin, Amy holds an M.Sc. in Psychoanalytic Psychotherapy from Trinity College Dublin.

Alex Bernson

Events Coordinator

Alex was born and raised in Milan, Italy. He studied P.R. and also earned a MSc in Marketing Management.

After working in marketing in Milan, Chicago, and Singapore he realized he wanted to focus on events. After experience with an espresso machine manufacturer he began an M.A. in International Events Management and relocated to London where he began to collaborate with WCE, first as volunteer coordinator and then as Events Coordinator.

Alex is curious by nature and enjoys reading and exploring the world.

Irene Beretta

Brand Manager

Irene Beretta grew up immersed in coffee in Seattle, Washington, getting her first cafe job in highschool. She has gone on to study coffee & society at Wesleyan University; work bar, train, and manage at cafes in Seattle, Portland and New York, and write and speak about coffee globally. She lives in Brooklyn, NY where she spends too much time exploring the city’s many culinary delights.
